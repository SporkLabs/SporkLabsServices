About

Who is Spork?

The Spork Labs new-media team consists of designers, modelers, animators, and many talented developers with specific expertise in mobile app development (Objc/Swift, Java, Ionic/Cordova, React Native, Titanium), advanced Javascript (AngularJS, EmberJS, Ext, Sencha, Node.js, Three.js), Ruby on Rails, Golang, PHP, HTML/CSS layout, user interface design, MySQL/PostgreSQL/NoSQL database administration and system operations.  The team boasts well over a century of collective application development experience, and includes a certified MySQL database administrator, industry-leading Ruby on Rails application architects, along with a strategic group of skilled programmers.

Jobs

Career Opportunities

Spork Labs is always looking for good talent, and offers compelling opportunities for career advancement.

Positions are available at Spork's Salt Lake City, UT office and at Spork Labs India's NUMA Bengaluru office.

Spork has a strong philosophy of hard work balanced with holistic health. Our Bengaluru office, particularly, is designed around principles of mental and spiritual balance. You may see us working long hours, but you'll also see us meditating, exercising (sometimes even singing!) Expectations are high, but in many ways we aren't building a traditional company—we're building a cooperative, a lifestyle, an extended family.

Our clients are part of that family. We deliver for them because we're deeply engaged in their goals and motives. We build their apps; we also attend their weddings, look at their baby pictures, and grow with them year after year. Our very first clients from 2006 are still clients today (and we aim to keep it that way!)

Spork requires exceptional JavaScript skills, with strong AngularJS and React experience. One or more of Ruby on Rails, Golang, Swift, Unreal, and Java are also preferred. Experienced Linux system administrators are valuable, with advanced AWS and/or Azure experience.
